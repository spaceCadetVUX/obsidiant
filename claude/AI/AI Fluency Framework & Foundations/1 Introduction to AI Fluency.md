## Video summary:
The speakers introduce a course about the **AI Fluency Framework**, which focuses on how **humans effectively collaborate with AI**, rather than just learning the technology itself. The goal is to help people treat AI as a **creative and problem-solving partner**, not simply a tool like a spellchecker.

Because AI is evolving very quickly, many people and organizations struggle to understand **how to use it properly**. The course aims to bridge the gap between **AI’s capabilities and people’s comfort level** by providing a stable framework instead of short-term tricks like prompt hacks.

The framework centers on **four core competencies (the “4 Ds”)**:

1. **Delegation** – deciding when a task should be done by humans vs. AI.
    
2. **Description** – communicating clearly with AI systems.
    
3. **Discernment** – evaluating and judging the quality and reliability of AI outputs.
    
4. **Diligence** – ensuring AI use is responsible, transparent, and accountable.
    

The course includes videos, exercises, and insights from Anthropic researchers to explain **AI capabilities and limitations**. Instead of teaching temporary prompt techniques, it focuses on **long-term skills and understanding** so learners can adapt to future AI developments.

**Outcome of the course:**  
Participants will gain a framework for working with AI, confidence in deciding when and how to use it, practical collaboration skills, and the ability to responsibly evaluate AI-generated results.



#### Key takeaways

- This course focuses on human-AI collaboration, not just understanding AI as a technology
- AI Fluency means engaging with AI systems effectively, efficiently, ethically, and safely
- The AI Fluency Framework centers on the "4D" competencies of Delegation, Description, Discernment and Diligence
- The goal is to develop lasting skills that remain relevant as AI technology evolves
- Effective AI collaboration requires both practical skills and a fundamental shift in how we think about working with AI

## Exercises

### Putting things into practice

_You'll need access to a language model (5-10 mins)_

Throughout this course, you'll practice what you're learning by working directly with a language model. While our examples feature Claude (Anthropic's AI assistant), you're welcome to work with any language model you prefer. The principles and skills we'll explore apply across different AI systems.

Getting started is simple and free:

- Claude: Visit [claude.ai](https://claude.ai/redirect/website.v1.1689607e-ad9e-4f0e-81a3-7d5a59a1059a) to create a free account
- No paid subscriptions required to complete course activities
- You can also use other AI chatbots as you prefer

New to Claude? No worries! We'll provide clear guidance with each exercise to help you get started.
